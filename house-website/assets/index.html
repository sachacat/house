Hi I’m Alice. I am British and come from Dorset, a place famous for its Jurrasic Coast.
It's also a place where old people go to die so moving to Amsterdam at a young age was exactly
what I was looking for.
I totally fell in love with the place and have made it my home now. This will be the 20th time I have moved!
Fingers crossed those nasty Brexiteers don’t ruin that for me!
I worked at Boom Chicago comedy theatre for about 10 years, as a waitress/bartender,
theatre manager and then I taught myself video editing and became a video editor there.
Since then I have done freelance editing work and was the kitchen manager at a pizza restaurant and lots of other hospitality jobs.
I am about to start working on a documentary about a friends journey into taoism, and I am also looking into going back to school to learn full stack web development. I took a 10 week bootcamp last year but it was too fast for me! I like bikram yoga, swimming and I am trying to enter into the Nijmegen vierdaagse this year. I’m very much looking forward to meeting you all and I am super excited and honoured that I could come and live with you all.
